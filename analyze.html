AOI Soil Analyzer

Pick an area. Get a map unit agronomic assessment.

Click the map to choose a spot and an area size. The analyzer reads every soil map unit in that area from the national soil survey and writes a plain-language assessment: the soils, agricultural suitability, water and drainage, limitations, and management notes. Then ask it questions about the area.

Click anywhere on the map to set the area. Cropland and survey-mapped land works best; open water returns no soils.
Desktop interpretation of generalized map unit data from the National Cooperative Soil Survey (SSURGO) via the National Cooperative Soil Survey (SSURGO). Ground truthing is needed for parcel-level or regulatory decisions. · Soil Intelligence
